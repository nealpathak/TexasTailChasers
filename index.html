About Us

Texas Tail Chasers is a local Galveston based fishing charter service, specializing in bay and nearshore fishing. Whether it be trophy Speckled Trout and Redfish you're after, or a variety of big fish and sharks, we cater to anglers of any skill level! We are a family friendly charter service that welcomes kids, families, bachelor/bachelorette parties, corporate trips, and tournaments.

(Trips include rods, reels, tackle, bait, and all fish cleaned and bagged)

Bay Trip (6AM - 12PM)

2 People - $500
3 People - $550
4 People - $600
For Tournaments - Add $100

Species of fish targeted:
Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder

Nearshore (6AM - 12PM)

2 - 4 People - $600




Species of fish targeted:
Sharks, Bull Reds, Jacks, Kingfish, Spanish Mackerel, Black Drum

What To Bring

Texas Saltwater Stamp Fishing License (Purchase Online)
Hat & Sunblock
Drinks & Food (No Glass Containers)
Ice Chest (Not for boat, Captain Jimmy will provide all ice chests on boat)

Deposits

Captain

Captain Jimmy

Captain Jimmy New is a U.S.C.G. licensed Captain who has been fishing the waters of Galveston Bay for over 20 years, and has also guided and competed in various tournaments all over the Galveston Bay Complex for over 5 years. He takes pride in putting his customers in the best spots for catching a possible fish of a lifetime, while also having a great and memorable experience on the water!
