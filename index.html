About Us

Texas Tail Chasers is a local Galveston based fishing charter service, specializing in bay and nearshore fishing. Whether it be trophy Speckled Trout and Redfish you're after, or a variety of big fish and sharks, we cater to anglers of any skill level! We are a family friendly charter service that welcomes kids, families, bachelor/bachelorette parties, corporate trips, and tournaments.

(Trips include Rods,reels,tackle, bait, and all fish cleaned and bagged)

Bay Trip (6AM - 12PM)

2 People - $500
3 People - $550
4 People - $600

Species of fish targeted:
Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder

Nearshore (6AM - 12PM)

2 - 4 People - $600



Species of fish targeted:
Sharks, Bull Reds, Jacks, Kingfish, Spanish Mackerel

Captain Jimmy

Captain

Captain Jimmy New is a U.S.C.G. licensed Captain who has been fishing the waters of Galveston Bay for over 20 years, has been guiding and competing in various tournaments all over the Galveston Bay Complex for over 5 years, and takes pride in putting his customers in the best spots for catching a possible fish of a lifetime, while having a great and memorable experience on the water!
